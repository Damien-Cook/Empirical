Empirical Discography

Empirical is a top-charting, global sensation who has recently risen to the Hot 100 charts from his debut single "Without You". At the age of only 17, Empirical has already mastered several instruments and gained a substantial fan base on various social media platforms such as Instagram and TikTok.

Empirical claimed in an interview to have been a small unsigned producer for several years before finally deciding to "shoot for the stars" and aim to be a globally known artist. Empirical believes that manifesting his goal has brought him to this point in his life and inspired him to push for higher and higher goals.

Empirical signed to Universal Music after the success of his beat "Meadows" which was exclusively uploaded to SoundCloud. The song had approximately 270k views when Universal Music found and offered Empirical the "Deal of a lifetime", as claimed by Empirical, to which he accepted, and claimed on Twitter was "The best decision I have ever made"

All of this has brought Empirical to where he is now, and with the new snippet "Tears" that he teased on Instagram, he could be pushing for even higher goals and records in the future!
