Empirical Discography

Empirical is a top charting 17 year old artist from England. He has been making music for almost 2 years and started from releasing "type beats" on SoundCloud where he initially gained popularity. During August of 2021, he released his debut single "Without You". This was the first time his audience had heard his voice, and it was his the beginning of his new found music career. In his park bench interview, Empirical claims "I always knew I had some kind of talent in music, wether it was as a producer, or an artist was something I had to discover for myself."
